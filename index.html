About Me

I'm currently an associate research professor with the College of Computer Science, Sichuan University, Chengdu, China. My research interests include unsupervised machine learning algorithms, image processing, deep learning algorithms and their applications.


I'm a PC member for several AI coferences AAAI 2018, AAAI 2019, AAAI 2020, AAAI 2021, IJCAI 2019, IJCAI 2020 and IJCAI 2021. I'm also a reviewer for several AI journals IEEE TPAMI, IEEE TNNLS, IEEE TCYB, IEEE TKDE, IEEE TFS, Pattern Recognition, and Neurocomputing.
